Quadrocopter

Description Here is a quadcopter drone which is a very popular design for ground, air and water unmanned aerial vehicles as well as motor control applications. It consists of a flight controller and four electronic speed controllers, one for each motor. The flight controller is equipped with a radio to receive flight commands provided by the pilot and the inertial measurement unit.
